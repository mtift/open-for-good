Contact

If you have questions about free software for your nonprofit or community organization, I’m happy to help. Whether you’re wondering where to start, trying to figure out if a tool fits your needs, or stuck on something technical, drop me a line.

You can reach me by email at info@matthewtift.com.

You can also find me on the web:

  • matthewtift.com (my personal site, built with Drupal)
  • Drupal.org (my profile in the Drupal community)

I’m not selling anything. This site exists because I think nonprofits deserve better tools, and I think free software provides them. If I can help you figure that out, I’d like to.
